Vehicles

A vehicle is a machine for transporting people or goods, with many types existing including cars, trains, ships, and bicycles.

Four Wheelers:

  1. Van
  2. Car
  3. Bus

Two Wheelers:

  • Scooter
  • Bicycle
  • Motorcycle

Functions:

Bus:
Buses are used for public transportation, school transport, and tourism.
Auto Rickshaw:
An auto rickshaw is a motorized, three-wheeled vehicle often used for short-distance public transportation in urban areas.
